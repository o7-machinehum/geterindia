Get er' India!

Ryan and Mohan met while working at the same company in Basel, Switzerland. They quickly bonded over beers, cooking and the Basel casino. What you'll find here is: Indian food, Canadian food or something in between.

I find myself constantly irritated by web bloat and the modern web in general. For whatever reason, recipe sites seem to be the pinnacle of this: littered with ads, auto-playing videos, and trackers bordering on malware. You won’t—and will never—find any of that here. Some recipes may contain “lore” sections; if you’re interested in reading more about where I got the recipe, you can find a link at the top. Otherwise, everything is directly to the point.
