##

### Ingredients

* Beef & Base
  * 500–700g stewing beef or white beans for veggie.
  * 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  * 2 tbsp tomato paste

* Spices
  * 1.5 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika (normal paprika works in a pinch)
  * 1 tsp caraway seeds (less important)
  * 1 bay leaf
  * Salt & pepper to taste
  * White pepper

* Vegetables
  * 1 large onion, sliced
  * 2 carrots, sliced
  * 1 parsnip, sliced (optional but traditional)
  * 1 green bell pepper, diced
  * 2 medium potatoes, diced

* Liquid
  * 1–1.2 L beef broth or water

### Instructions

  - **Cook the base:** In a large pot, heat the oil and all vegetables (except potatoes). Cook until soft, with a nice bite. If you're using beef add here as well.
  - **Activate aromatics:** Add garlic, cook until fragrant (2mins)
  - **Activate Spice:** Push everything off to the side of the pan, add more oil to blank side, increase heat, add spices into oil and activate them.
  - **Make broth:** Add beef broth, add potatoes. Light boil until potatoes cooked.
  - **Finish:** Add while beans, season with salt, pepper and white pepper to taste
